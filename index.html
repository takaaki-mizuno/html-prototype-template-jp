CEO Profile

Takaaki Mizuno

CEO & Founder

After graduating from the University of Tokyo Graduate School, Takaaki worked in software development and development team management at companies including Hatena, Baidu, and DeNA.

At Baidu, he joined as the first Japanese developer and won the Baidu Person of the Year award representing the Shanghai development center. At DeNA, he contributed to establishing development bases in Vietnam and Singapore.

His expertise includes software design and development including AI model development, building and managing multinational development teams, and resolving crisis projects.

Since 2014, he has been based in Singapore, supporting new business ventures in Japan and Southeast Asia, providing initial development support for startups, solving development-related problems, and building overseas development teams.
